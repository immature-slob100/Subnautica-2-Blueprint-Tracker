## ⚙️ How It Works

The dashboard reads your game state to find the blueprints you unlocked. It displays these items in a list. You track your progress by clicking items as you gather resources.

### Tracking Blueprints
The main view shows a list of every item you can craft. You see the required materials next to each blueprint. The application highlights items when you possess enough resources to build them. This helps you plan your next move in the game.

### Technology Progress
The technology tab shows your full upgrade path. You see which items require specific blueprints or depths to unlock. It marks completed tasks so you know your current stage in the game. Use this to avoid missing key upgrades during your dives.

### Managing Resources
The resource tracker lists the items you need for your current projects. When you check a blueprint as started, the app subtracts those items from your total requirements. This makes inventory management much easier while you explore the surface or the ocean floor.
